Jonathan is a Computer Engineer graduate with a passion for Embedded Systems and Software Engineering. He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Engineering from Florida International University (FIU), with concentrations in Embedded Systems, Software Design, and Electronics.

During his time at FIU, he participated in the Honors College program and performed research for the Open Human Interface Design (OpenHID) laboratory (soon to be renamed to NUILabs). His main areas of research were Augmented Reality and Multimodal Interaction. He co-authored several reseach papers, conducted various Institutional-Review-Board (IRB) approved experiments during his time with OpenHID and worked on various lab projects, such as BioBrace VR and a Unity-based Holographic spatial environment for the Microsoft HoloLens.
